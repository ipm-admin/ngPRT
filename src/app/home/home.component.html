Why pesticiderisk.org?

The Pesticide Risk Tool estimates the risk of negative impacts of pesticide applications. Ready access to this information will help reduce adverse impacts and improve environmental stewardship.

What's new?

The Pesticide Risk Tool has been updated to include a new User-Defined Products feature. This allows international users to analyze products that are not registered with the US EPA. The workspace has also been improved to enhance user experience.
