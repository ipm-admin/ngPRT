Information

The PRT Bulk Analysis tool performs a full risk analysis on many blocks (fields) of pesticide data.

Options
  • Select the risk indices you'd like to include, or choose a preset.
  • Choose the level of detail you'd like to see in results by selecting
    • Block-Level - Outputs risk result summaries for each block.
    • Product-Level - Outputs risk result summaries for each active ingredient identified for each pesticide application, organized by block.
Note on the PSI preset

Only a subset of risk indices apply to potatoes. Selecting PSI removes the level of detail option (forces block-level results) and prompts you to indicate the region the data applies to and By selecting a region, you will see the reduction in the number of high risk scores based on an estimated number of high risk scores per block by region without using IPM practices. If a region isn't selected, you will only see high risk scores per block.
